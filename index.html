How it all began...

Eunike Design Studio was founded by Jennifer Eunike, a calligraphy enthusiast and freelance graphic designer who is based in Jakarta, Indonesia. She fell in love with calligraphy in early 2015 but only began learning and practicing obsessively since early 2016.. Having been criticized throughout her school years for her ugly handwriting and limited drawing skills, she found  calligraphy as a new, relaxing way to re-learn how to write. She has also been practicing loose watercolor as it goes perfectly with calligraphy, and constantly finding ways to combine her graphic design skills with these arts.
